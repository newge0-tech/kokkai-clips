Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-14

This privacy policy describes how kokkai-clips ("the App") handles information when it publishes short-form video clips derived from publicly available Japanese parliamentary proceedings (国会審議) to the operator's own TikTok and YouTube channels.

1. Scope

The App is a personal, automated publishing tool operated by a single developer. It uploads videos to a single TikTok account (@kokkai_archive) and a single YouTube channel owned by the developer. The App does not serve third-party end users and does not offer any user-facing interface to the public.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information from end users. Specifically:

  • No account registration, login, or contact form is provided to third parties.
  • No cookies, analytics scripts, or tracking pixels are used on this website.
  • No IP addresses, device identifiers, or usage logs are collected from visitors to this site.

The App only processes publicly broadcast parliamentary proceedings retrieved from official Japanese House of Representatives and House of Councillors internet broadcast services.

3. Information Shared With Third Parties

The App transmits the following to the TikTok Content Posting API and YouTube Data API, solely for the purpose of publishing videos to the developer's own accounts:

  • Video file (MP4) derived from public parliamentary recordings
  • Video title, description, and hashtags
  • OAuth access tokens obtained through the TikTok Login Kit and Google OAuth 2.0

These transmissions are governed by the respective platforms' privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Retention

OAuth access tokens and video files are stored locally on the developer's machine. Processed clips are retained for up to 7 days after upload, then deleted. Raw source recordings are retained for up to 3 days.

5. AI-Generated Content Disclosure

Published videos may contain AI-generated synthesized speech (VOICEVOX) in the opening "hook" segment. This is disclosed in each video's description where applicable.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. No information is knowingly collected from anyone.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

8. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please contact the channel operator through direct messages on TikTok or YouTube.
